# **Customer Retention \& Churn Analysis Dashboard**



#### **Overview**

This project focuses on analyzing customer churn and retention patterns using an interactive Power BI dashboard. The goal is to identify factors influencing customer churn and provide insights that help businesses improve customer retention.



This project was completed as part of **\*\*Task 2 – Customer Retention \& Churn Analysis\*\*** during my internship with **Future Interns.**



#### **Objectives**

\- Understand customer churn behavior

\- Identify factors contributing to customer attrition

\- Analyze customer retention trends

\- Provide actionable insights for improving retention strategies



#### **Tools Used**

\- Power BI

\- Microsoft Excel

\- Data Visualization

\- Business Analytics



#### **Key Dashboard Features**

\- Total Customers KPI

\- Churn vs Retained Customers Analysis

\- Churn by Contract Type

\- Churn by Payment Method

\- Churn by Internet Service

\- Customer Tenure Distribution

\- Support Ticket Trends

\- Monthly Charges Analysis



##### **Key Insights**

\- Customers with month-to-month contracts show higher churn rates.

\- Customers using fiber optic internet services have higher churn patterns.

\- Most churn occurs during the early customer tenure period.

\- Support ticket trends indicate potential service-related churn factors.
